Services

Empowering Agribusiness with Digital Funding Solutions

The AgriFund Web Platform revolutionizes agribusiness funding by providing an online marketplace connecting small farmers and investors. This platform streamlines the process of securing funding for agricultural projects, enabling farmers to apply for loans and investors to find lucrative investment opportunities. Built with Python, Django, and MySQL, AgriFund ensures a seamless and secure experience for users, empowering them to grow their agricultural ventures with confidence. The platform will cater to small farmers and investors interested in supporting agribusiness ventures, offering a convenient and accessible funding solution.


How to Use Our Platform

Navigate through AgriFund with ease

Create a project

The farmer fills out a form with project details, which is then saved to the database and listed on the platform.

- Farmer seeking funding for agricultural project

Apply for a loan

The farmer submits a loan application form, which is linked to their project and saved to the database.

- Farmer access the loan form to secure funding

Browse Projects

The investor views a list of projects with basic details on the project listings page.

- Investor browses through available projects to find investment opportunities

Express Interest

The investor fills out a form with their contact details, which triggers a notification to the farmer and updates the project's status to "Interested."

- Investor express interest in a project so that the farmer knows they are willing to fund it.

Receive Notifications

The farmer receives an email with the investor's contact details and the project's status is updated.

- Farmer receives notification when an investor is interested in their project for follow up on funding.
